How to Register your Organization on Finndemy

Step 1: Sign Up

  • Visit https://finndemy.com
  • Click on the Sign Up button.
  • Register using your email address or Google account.

🔐 Step 2: Log In

  • After registering, log in with your credentials.
  • You will be redirected to your account dashboard.

📌 Step 3: Access Business Plans

  • In the top menu, click FINNDEMY PLANS.

✅ Step 4: Fill Out the Business Registration Form

Once you've clicked FINNDEMY PLANS, you will land on the Business Registration form page (as shown in the image).

  1. First Name – Your first name.
  2. Last Name – Your surname.
  3. Enter your work email address – Must be the email you used to sign up on Finndemy.
  4. Your phone number – Provide a valid phone number.
  5. Organization name – Enter your company/business name.
  6. Number of seats (Employees) – Indicate how many employee accounts you'll need.

📤 Step 5: Submit

  • Click the “Register Business” button.
  • Your registration will be submitted for review.

✅ Step 6: Registration Confirmation

Once you submit the form, you'll see a confirmation message like this:

  • Your submitted details (Business Name, Contact Info, Seats, etc.) will be displayed.
  • 📩 Status Message:
  • ⏳ “Your group is being processed. We'll email you when ready.”

This means the Finndemy team is reviewing your registration. You’ll receive an email once your group account is set up and active.

How to Create a Group for Your Organization

What Is a Group in This Context?
In this context, a Group refers to an organizational or team-based enrollment structure within Finndemy. It allows an organization (such as a company, school, or institution) to purchase a bulk course license and manage access for multiple members under a single group account.

Key benefits of Groups include:

  • Centralized billing
  • Group-level course assignments
  • Easy monitoring of member progress
  • Admin control over adding/removing users

Step 1: Log in as Group Leader/Admin

  • Use the email you registered during checkout where you bought a Finndemy Plan.
  • Go to the platform dashboard.

Step 2: Navigate to Your Group

  • Click on “Group Dashboard”.

Step 3: Manage the Organization

  • In the dropdown beside “Change Organization,” select the correct organization if you belong to more than one.

Click the blue “Manage Organization” button.

This will open the management panel where you can:

  • Add a Team
  • Manage Teams

Click on “Add Team”

After successfully creating a team, move on to “How to Add Team Members”

Ensure a Department-Based Structure

An organization can have as many teams as necessary. The organization below has at least four distinct teams:

  • Governance Team
  • Legal & Compliance Team
  • Pension Team
  • Product Development Team

Each department is likely to have its own set of team members and leaders, requiring tailored training content. To support them:

  • You need seats for team leaders to manage courses.
  • You need seats for each team member to access learning resources.

How to Add Team Members (After Team Creation)

Step 1: Go to the Group Dashboard

  • Ensure you’re in the correct organization (e.g., Busy Beezz) using the dropdown labeled "Change Organization".

Step 2: Locate the Team You Want to Manage

  • Scroll through the list of teams under "Team Name".
  • Each row represents a different department/team (e.g., Governance Team, Pension Team, etc.).

Step 3: Click on "Team Members"

  • Under the "Manage Teams" column, click the "Team Members" button next to the relevant team.
  • 🔴 Tip: This is highlighted in red in the screenshot above.

1. Add Team Members (Single Entry)

🔹 Option 1: Add a New Member Manually

  1. Click “Add Team Member”
  1. Enter Details in the Form:
  • First Name (required)
  • Last Name
  • Username (required)
  • Email (optional but recommended)
  • Password: A system-generated password will be shown (you may customize it if allowed)

➕ This will create a new user and add them directly to the selected team.

🔹 Option 2: Add an Existing User

  1. Check the box that says “Team Member already exists?”
  1. Type their name or email address in the search field.
  2. Select from the suggested matches (e.g., “Marsh (MarshZenderman)” or an email address).
  3. Click “Save”

🔁 This links an existing platform user to this specific team — without creating a duplicate.

✅ Best for quick, individual entries.

2. Import List (Bulk Entry via CSV)

Use this method to upload multiple team members at once.

  1. Click “Import List”.
  1. On the next page, click “Download CSV template”.
  1. Open the downloaded CSV (e.g., GroupsPlusUsers.csv).
  1. Fill in the member details as required.
  1. Return to the site and click “Browse” to upload the file.
  1. Click “Save” to complete the import.

✅ Best for adding many members efficiently.
